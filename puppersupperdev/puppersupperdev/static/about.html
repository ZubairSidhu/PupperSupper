What we are

Humans have spent a lot of time trying to improve their health. They even have componenets to measure their health but what about man's best friend? We decided to make a project dedicated to helping animals live a long lasting life through what not to feed it so it does not die. It also has the ability to measure how much an animal exercises.

What you need

The first thing you will need of course is your dog. We want to continue updating wellness and fitness on other animals but for now we are working on just dogs. You need internet(which you are probably using) to run the Wellness component

Wellness Component

The Wellness componenet is an application that shows what foods are deadly or unhealthy for your dog
